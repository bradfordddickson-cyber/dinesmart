How It Works

Simple for students.
Powerful for dining services.

1

Student sets preferences

Allergens, dietary labels, and personal notes — saved privately in their browser.

2

Menu is filtered instantly

Every dish is scanned. Safe items are highlighted. Risks are flagged with clear warnings.

3

AI answers their questions

"What's high-protein and nut-free for lunch today?" Claude responds in seconds.

4

Dining staff update menus

Admin portal lets staff update menus daily. Changes are live for students immediately.

Student asks:

"What can I eat for dinner if I'm allergic to nuts and avoiding gluten?"

Claude responds:

"Tonight at the grill you can safely have the Grilled Chicken and French Fries — both gluten-free and nut-free. For protein, the Korean Beef Bulgogi is also safe if you skip the sauce. Avoid the pasta bar tonight."
