Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026  ·  DineSmart Pilot Program  ·  Harvard College

Pilot Program Notice: DineSmart is currently operating as an independent demo pilot. It is not an official Harvard University service. This policy describes our current data practices during the pilot phase.

1. Who We Are

DineSmart is an independent software tool designed to help Harvard College students navigate dining hall menus and manage food allergy information. We are not affiliated with or endorsed by Harvard University Dining Services (HUDS) or Harvard University.

For questions about this policy, contact us at: privacy@dinesmart.app

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide

  • Dietary preferences and allergen restrictions — stored locally in your browser only (localStorage). This data never leaves your device unless you use the AI recommendation feature.
  • AI chat messages — when you use the AI meal recommendation feature, your message (including any allergen preferences you've set) is sent to our server and forwarded to Anthropic's Claude API to generate a response.

Information collected automatically

  • Server logs — when you use the AI recommendation feature, our server records a log entry containing: timestamp, IP address, and the number of messages sent. We do not log the content of your messages in our audit log.
  • Standard web server logs — your IP address and browser type may be captured in standard server access logs.

Information we do NOT collect

  • We do not collect your name, Harvard ID (HUID), or email address in the current pilot.
  • We do not use cookies for tracking.
  • We do not collect payment information.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide AI-powered meal recommendations based on your stated preferences.
  • To maintain service security and prevent abuse (rate limiting).
  • To improve the service during the pilot phase.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

Anthropic (Claude AI)

When you use the AI recommendation feature, your message is transmitted to Anthropic, Inc. to generate a response. Anthropic's privacy policy governs how they handle this data. We recommend reviewing Anthropic's Privacy Policy.

Important: Do not include sensitive personal information (such as medical records or student ID numbers) in your AI chat messages.

Vercel (Hosting)

This application is hosted on Vercel, Inc. Standard server logs may be retained by Vercel per their privacy policy.

5. Data Storage and Retention

  • Browser localStorage: Your dietary preferences are stored locally in your browser and remain there until you clear your browser data. We have no access to this data.
  • Server audit logs: Logs of AI recommendation requests (timestamp, IP, message count) are retained for up to 90 days for security purposes.
  • Menu data: Dining hall menu information entered by administrators is stored in your browser's localStorage. It is not stored on our servers.

6. FERPA

The Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act (FERPA) protects the privacy of student education records. During the current pilot:

  • DineSmart does not collect or store Harvard ID numbers (HUIDs), student names, or academic records.
  • Allergen and dietary preference data is stored only in your local browser and is not transmitted to our servers except when included in an AI recommendation request.
  • A full FERPA compliance review will be conducted prior to any official Harvard partnership or deployment that involves authentication through HarvardKey.

7. Health Information

DineSmart is not a medical service. Allergen and dietary information displayed in this application is provided for convenience only and may not be complete or accurate. Always verify allergen information directly with Harvard dining staff before consuming any food.

We strongly encourage students with severe food allergies or medical dietary requirements to speak directly with HUDS nutrition staff.

8. Your Rights

  • Access: You may request information about what data we hold about you.
  • Deletion: You may request deletion of any server-side logs associated with your IP address.
  • Local data: You can delete your locally stored preferences at any time by clearing your browser's localStorage for this site.

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@dinesmart.app.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the service evolves. Material changes will be noted at the top of this page with a new "Last updated" date. Continued use of DineSmart after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
